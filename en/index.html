Passenger Information

Check-in: Check-in closes 30 minutes before departure. We kindly ask all passengers to arrive at check-in one hour before departure whenever possible.

On-board service: Our on-board service offers complimentary meals, beverages and free Wi-Fi for guests in all carriage classes.

Luggage: There is no limit on the number of luggage items on EuroRail One trains. However, we ask that luggage larger than two large airline suitcases be registered.

Accessibility: Our trains are wheelchair accessible and offer special seating for passengers with additional needs. Mobility assistance can be easily requested during booking. You don’t need to worry about anything – we will contact you.

Delays: In the event of delays, we will inform you immediately via our website, app, email or at the station.
